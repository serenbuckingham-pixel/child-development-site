Child and Young Person Development Milestones (UK)

Birth – 1 Year

Physical Development

  • Head control by 3 months
  • Rolling over by 6 months
  • Sitting independently by 8 months
  • Pulling to stand by 12 months

Speech, Language & Communication

  • Cooing and babbling by 3–6 months
  • Responds to familiar voices
  • First words by 12 months

Cognitive Development

  • Recognises familiar faces
  • Explores objects with hands and mouth
  • Curiosity about surroundings

Personal, Social & Emotional Development

  • Smiles in response to others
  • Forms attachment to caregivers
  • Shows basic emotions

1 – 3 Years (Toddlers)

Physical Development

  • Walking independently by 18 months
  • Running and climbing improves by 2 years
  • Uses utensils for eating

Speech, Language & Communication

  • Forms simple sentences by 2 years
  • Understands and follows simple instructions
  • Expanding vocabulary

Cognitive Development

  • Imaginative play
  • Recognises colours, shapes, and sizes
  • Solves simple puzzles

Personal, Social & Emotional Development

  • Self-care: washing hands, feeding with guidance
  • Parallel play and sharing
  • Expresses feelings clearly

3 – 5 Years (Preschool)

Physical Development

  • Runs, hops, and jumps with balance
  • Draws shapes, cuts with scissors
  • Dresses and feeds independently

Speech, Language & Communication

  • Uses full sentences with grammar
  • Retells stories and enjoys rhymes
  • Understands plurals and tenses

Cognitive Development

  • Counts and recognises numbers
  • Sorts and classifies objects
  • Role-playing and imaginative play

Personal, Social & Emotional Development

  • Forms friendships and plays in groups
  • Follows routines and rules
  • Shows empathy to others

5 – 11 Years (Primary School)

Physical Development

  • Participates in sports, improves strength and agility
  • Writes neatly and draws detailed pictures
  • Hand-eye coordination for games

Speech, Language & Communication

  • Explains ideas clearly in full sentences
  • Reads fluently and writes coherent text
  • Understands jokes and idioms

Cognitive Development

  • Solves problems independently
  • Understands cause and effect
  • Explores topics of interest

Personal, Social & Emotional Development

  • Builds friendships and cooperates with peers
  • Recognises own strengths and interests
  • Follows rules and understands consequences

12 – 19 Years (Teenagers / Secondary School)

Physical Development

  • Experiences puberty and growth spurts
  • Improved strength and coordination
  • Supports participation in sports

Speech, Language & Communication

  • Discusses complex ideas and opinions
  • Can debate and explain reasoning
  • Uses digital media to communicate effectively

Cognitive Development

  • Critical thinking and problem-solving
  • Plans for education, career, and personal goals
  • Understands hypothetical and moral concepts

Personal, Social & Emotional Development

  • Develops self-identity and personal values
  • Forms close friendships and romantic relationships
  • Manages stress, conflict, and complex feelings
